About US

The journey of SAV SPACE started way back when Arun and team worked on small commercial projects for own ventures. Since the projects were carried out for own projects, the team was extremely conscious about quality of work and cost aspects. It is that experience and connection with vendors which is leveraged by SAV SPACE today to bring value to customers.

Today the team together has completed many iconic projects including turnkey commercial and residential ones.
