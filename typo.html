Five Step Process

Requirement Gathering:

Projects are designed after we understand clientsâ€™ needs requirement of the complete project. We prefer these discussions to happen at the site itself so client and designer understand it completely. Our team will also gather information regarding timelines and budget at this stage

Concept Proposal:

At the second stage our trained design team will present the concept and ideas tailored to you. Once you have approved the concept, we begin quickly to move in a direction to make it happen.

Design:

At this stage, our design team will give you the designs of each space. We will also give you specific material, finishes and furnishing options. Sample where ever required will be presented for your approval and specifications of your project will be listed at this stage. Based on the approvals, we will present final budget to you.

Installation:

At this stage the work will begin and your will start getting periodic update from our team at every stage.

Delivery:

We will deliver the project as per the timelines fixed. You will see your space transformed as per your needs

Post Delivery Visit:

After 2 weeks of the delivery our person will visit the site again to check for any issues from client side. This is a proactive step as we know that you will be 100 percent satisfied to welcome our team member with a smiling face!
