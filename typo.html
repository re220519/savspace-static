Six Step Process

Requirement Gathering:

SAV SPACE team initiate project design only after understanding complete requirements of the client. We prefer these discussions to happen at the site itself so that client and the designer understand it completely. Our team will also gather information regarding timelines and budget at this stage.

Concept Proposal:

At the second stage our trained design team will present the concept and ideas tailored for you. Once you approve the concept, we will quickly to move to make it happen.

Design:

At this stage, our design team will give you the designs of each space. We will also give you specific material, finishing and furnishing options. Sample where ever required will be presented for your approval and specifications of your project will be listed at this stage. Based on the approvals, we will present final budget to you.

Installation:

At this stage the work will begin and you will start getting periodic update from our team.

Delivery:

We will deliver the project as per the timelines fixed. You will see your space transformed as per your needs.

Post Delivery Visit:

After 2 weeks of the delivery our person will visit the site again to check for any issues from client side. This is a proactive step as we know that you will be 100 percent satisfied to welcome our team member with a smiling face!
