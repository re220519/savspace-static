Economy Projects

Budget constrains are stopping you from materialising your dream? We can help you. Our team can suggest alternative options for material and design, that will bring down the cost. We will upfront inform you about quality of the materials used so that you take the correct decision.

Economy Projects are those where cost implications are prioritised above the quality and durability. For example, if the client wants less durable and cost-effective solutions, we provide them. For these projects we use alternatives such as engineering wood instead of plywood and unbranded paints. As a quality conscious organisation, we provide prior information to customer about the materials, its quality and durability.
